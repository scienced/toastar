Our culture

Roadmunk was inspired by a problem our founders experienced personally: building roadmaps was time-consuming and finicky. Our product was created from a genuine desire to solve this problem for our users, and we bring the same sense of passion and empathy to everything we do. We believe in superior design, uncompromising code quality, and setting ambitious goals. Our team is made up of talented individuals who are highly motivated to grow our company and themselves—and have fun in the process.

Roadmunk Values

Life at Roadmunk

Culture is not an afterthought at our company. It’s a core part of who we are.


Growth

Be part of a fast-growing startup. Hone your talent and expand your industry knowledge. Our culture empowers people to debate ideas, take risks and learn in a safe, positive way.


Technology

Our stack is always improving. We run our platform on top of Docker and AWS, and leverage Node.js, MongoDB & KnockoutJS. We use the latest productivity tools, including Slack, Trello and Intercom. And of course, Roadmunk.


Fun + Flexibility

We have team events every month, from bowling to baseball to go-karting. We’ve got a well-stocked snack fridge and endless coffee. And we get the work/life dilemma. No one is watching the clock here.


Getaways

Winter blahs kill productivity. We’ve taken our entire company to countries like Costa Rica and Nicaragua for a few weeks of fresh perspective and cervezas in February.

Our offices

Toronto

Our operations team works out of a brick-and-beam office in downtown Toronto. We’ve got easy access to the TTC, endless lunch options, and close proximity to both gourmet and budget coffee spots.

Kitchener

Our engineering team is based in downtown Kitchener-Waterloo. Our roomy, newly renovated space is located under a yoga studio and includes two kitchens and a mini gym.

London, UK

Our London satellite office provides customer success to European time zones, helping us provide round-the-clock support to clients around the world.
